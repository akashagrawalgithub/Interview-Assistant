Refund Policy

Last updated: January 14, 2026

1. No Refund Policy

Due to the nature of our digital products and services, InterviewAssistant operates a strict no-refund policy. All sales are final, and we do not offer refunds or credits for any partial subscription periods or unused digital goods.

2. Evaluating the Service

We encourage all users to use our Free plan to evaluate the features and compatibility of InterviewAssistant with their workflow before committing to a paid subscription.

3. Cancellation of Subscription

You can cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings. Upon cancellation, you will continue to have access to the service through the end of your current billing period. No further charges will be applied after cancellation, but no previous payments will be refunded.

4. Exceptional Circumstances

In very limited and exceptional circumstances (e.g., duplicate charges or double billing), you may contact our support team at support@interviewassistant.co.in to review your case for a potential manual adjustment.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our No Refund Policy, please contact us at support@interviewassistant.co.in.
